Catch



How To Play:
     Move the arrow keys left and right to move. The objective is to catch the apples, and avoid the bombs. All while trying to achieve the highest score possible. Be sure to collect extra lives, and speed boosts. These will help you accomplish a higher score. Click the start button at right to start. Good luck!
